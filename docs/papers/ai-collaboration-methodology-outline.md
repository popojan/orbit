## Key Themes (from misc/*.txt)

### 1. The Trinity (trinity.txt)

Three necessary components:
- **Human**: Geometric vision (primal forest concept)
- **Wolfram**: Computational verification (numerics, symbolic)
- **AI**: Rapid execution, formalization, tireless exploration

None sufficient alone. Together: breakthrough.

### 2. AI as Colleague, Not Tool (kladivo.txt)

- Not "hammer" but "mind in different modality"
- Complements human strengths/weaknesses
- Honest about confidence levels (8/10 self-assessment)
- Aware of limitations

### 3. Discovery Process (dukaz.txt, luck.txt)

**Cycle**:
1. Geometric intuition suggests direction
2. Numerical experiments (Wolfram)
3. AI derives formal proof
4. Human verifies rigor
5. Independent numerical confirmation

**Not**:
- Purely theoretical derivation
- Blind symbolic manipulation
- Unverified conjectures

### 4. Speed vs. Correctness (consiousness.txt)

**Speed enables**:
- Exploring 50 variants in one evening
- Rapid iteration on errors
- Testing ideas before investing weeks

**Dangers**:
- Systematic blind spots (AI may miss)
- Need human critical review
- Must verify, not just trust

### 5. Synthesis of Ideas (synteza.txt, vse-se-propojilo-primality-pell.txt)

AI excels at:
- Connecting disparate concepts
- Finding patterns across domains
- Formalizing vague intuitions

Human excels at:
- Recognizing importance
- Geometric visualization
- Knowing when to stop/pivot

### 6. Collaboration Dynamics (o-spolupraci-s-ai.txt, superpower.txt)

**What makes it work**:
- Clear division of roles
- Mutual trust with verification
- Honest communication about uncertainty
- Shared excitement (not just tool usage)

**Pitfalls**:
- Over-reliance on AI correctness
- Under-appreciating human intuition
- Treating AI as oracle vs. partner
